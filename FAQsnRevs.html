Frequently Asked Questions

1What services do you offer at Cahili Birthing Facility?

We offer a range of maternity services including prenatal care, natural birthing, postpartum care, and newborn care.

2What is the cost of a birthing package?

Our birthing packages range from 3,000 to 4,000 pesos, but prices may vary depending on specific needs and the selected package. Negotiations can be made based on individual requirements.

3Is Cahili Birthing Facility open 24/7?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency support and birthing services to ensure that we are always available when you need us.

4Do you offer prenatal classes?

Yes, we offer prenatal classes to prepare mothers for childbirth, covering topics like breathing techniques, labor stages, and newborn care.

5What are the qualifications of your medical staff?

Our facility is staffed by licensed and experienced midwives, nurses, and obstetricians to ensure the safety and comfort of both mother and baby.

6Do you accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept several health insurance plans. Please contact us for details on coverage and payment options.

7Can my partner or family member be with me during labor?

Absolutely! We encourage the presence of your partner or a support person during labor and delivery for emotional support.

8What is the process for scheduling a birthing appointment?

You can notify us of your planned appointment by calling us directly or through our Facebook page. However, you will still need to visit the facility in person to finalize the booking.

9What is included in the postpartum care package?

Our postpartum care package includes follow-up checkups, breastfeeding support, newborn screenings, and guidance for new mothers.
